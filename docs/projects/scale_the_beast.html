Scale the Beast

(2024)

Roles: Programmer, Level Designer

"Scale the Beast" is an action platformer developed during the GMTK Game Jam 2024 in just 4 days. Working with the same team as our previous project, we focused on implementing code that could be reused effectively. The game emphasizes separating the functionality of classes into distinct components that aren't tightly coupled, ensuring flexibility and maintainability in the design.

What I Did

  • Focused on programming with an emphasis on reusability and separation of concerns within the codebase.
  • Designed levels to challenge players while providing a sense of progression and adventure.
  • Created the intro scene on the cliff, featuring a dramatic dragon entrance that transitions seamlessly to the title screen.

Things I Learned

  • The importance of reusability in code, and how decoupling components enhances flexibility and maintainability.
  • The significance of playtesting, as a missing spike in the level design led to some players missing a crucial lesson for progression.
